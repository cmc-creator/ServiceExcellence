Requirements + Support

What learners need and where to get help.

System Requirements

  • Modern Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox browser
  • Stable internet connection for tracking and submission
  • Desktop or tablet recommended for best readability
  • Audio optional for scenario immersion

Need Assistance?

  • Login or access issues: contact your facility training lead
  • Completion not recorded: retry submission, then report immediately
  • Technical issue during session: capture screenshot and time of error
  • Role track mismatch: notify your manager before completing

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from learners and supervisors.

Do I need to complete this in one sitting?

You can pause and resume, but complete all modules and submit attestation before your deadline.

How do I know I passed?

Your final score is shown at completion. A passing score is 80% or higher.

What if my completion does not appear?

Submit once more, then contact your training lead with your role, timestamp, and any error message.

Ready to begin this year's training?
