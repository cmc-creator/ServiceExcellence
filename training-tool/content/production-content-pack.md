# Production Content Pack

## 1. Experience Copy Pack
- Welcome headline and subhead variants
- Role track labels and microcopy
- Scenario prompt text and feedback text
- Assessment prompt and answer text
- Completion and remediation text

## 2. Motion and Interaction Spec
- Panel transitions: 450 to 650ms rise and fade
- Choice feedback reveal: 180 to 220ms
- Badge unlock pulse: 320ms
- Timer urgency shift at 15 seconds remaining

## 3. Visual Design Token Pack
- Palette: deep navy, marine blue, warm gold, aqua mint accents
- Surface: layered glass cards with subtle border glow
- Texture: soft procedural noise and gradient atmosphere
- Typography: Space Grotesk + Outfit

## 4. Audio and Voiceover Pack
- Intro and module transition narration scripts
- Optional subtle ambient background loop
- Success and warning sound cues at low volume
- Caption and transcript text files for accessibility

## 5. Compliance Pack
- Policy citation map per scenario
- Mandatory annual attestation language
- Reporting hotline and speak-up references
- Version and effective-date metadata

## 6. LMS Pack
- SCORM manifest template included
- xAPI event model included
- Completion submit call in runtime
- Score and pass/fail mapping from final assessment

## 7. QA Checklist
- Accessibility checks: keyboard navigation, contrast, focus states
- Content checks: legal and HR sign-off
- Tracking checks: completion, score, and commit behavior
- Device checks: desktop and mobile responsiveness

## 8. Launch Checklist
- Pilot with representative users across role tracks
- Capture confusion points and missed questions
- Adjust wording and scenario clarity
- Publish to LMS production and monitor analytics weekly for first month
