# Master Training Script - Annual Service Excellence and Code of Conduct

## Runtime
- Total duration: 45 to 60 minutes
- Format: self-paced interactive annual training
- Required pass threshold: 80 percent on final assessment

## Module 1 - Welcome (3 to 5 minutes)
Opening line:
"Welcome to the Excellence Vault. In this annual mission, every choice you make strengthens trust, safety, and integrity at Destint Springs Healthcare."

Narration points:
- Explain score, streak, and badge mechanics.
- Confirm role track selection.
- Explain that training is scenario-driven and practical.

Humor-safe line:
"Good news: no pop quizzes with trick math. Better news: good decisions still get rewards."

## Module 2 - Service Mindset (12 to 15 minutes)
Transition line:
"Service excellence is not a slogan, it is a sequence of repeatable behaviors in pressure moments."

Narration points:
- Acknowledge, clarify, and follow through.
- Professionalism and visible ownership.
- Team communication with closed-loop handoffs.
- Patient and family trust as a measurable outcome.

Humor-safe line:
"If your plan depends on telepathy, we should switch plans."

## Module 3 - Conduct Compass (12 to 15 minutes)
Transition line:
"Code of Conduct is how we protect people, colleagues, and the organization when situations get complicated."

Narration points:
- Reporting pathways and non-retaliation.
- Privacy and confidentiality boundaries.
- Conflicts of interest and gift handling.
- Documentation integrity and billing ethics.

Humor-safe line:
"If someone says 'just this once,' policy still says 'not this once either.'"

## Module 4 - Scenario Arena (10 to 12 minutes)
Transition line:
"Now the decision lab opens. You will choose responses and see immediate impact."

Narration points:
- Encourage pause before each choice.
- Explain feedback and score impact.
- Reinforce role-specific context.

Coaching line:
"Best practice is usually empathy plus clarity plus ownership."

## Module 5 - Lightning + Final Certification (10 to 12 minutes)
Transition line:
"You are in the final stretch. Speed matters, standards matter more."

Narration points:
- 60-second lightning challenge.
- 25-question final assessment.
- Required attestation and completion submission.

Closing line:
"Thank you for protecting excellence, conduct, and care quality every day."

## Remediation Script (if below pass threshold)
"You are close. Complete the targeted review and retake the final assessment to finalize annual status."

## Manager Follow-Up Prompt
"After completion, discuss one service behavior and one conduct behavior each team member will improve this quarter."
