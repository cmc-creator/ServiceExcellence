# Department Scenario Packs

These packs turn the shared annual training into short, department-specific refreshers.

## How to Use

- Pick the learner's department track.
- Use the pack's scenarios as the post-training coaching or monthly refresher set.
- Keep the shared annual modules as the baseline, then layer the department pack on top.

## Clinical Staff Pack

Focus areas:

- Handoff safety
- Bedside communication
- Documentation integrity
- Privacy in care areas

Starter scenarios:

1. Observation Handoff Conflict
- Setup: Bedside report conflicts with the active precaution order.
- Skill: Closed-loop handoff and immediate reconciliation.

2. Privacy in the Treatment Area
- Setup: A patient details conversation carries into a public hallway.
- Skill: Immediate redirection to a private space.

3. Service Recovery After Missed Follow-Up
- Setup: A patient asks why a promised update never happened.
- Skill: Own the miss, correct it, and close the loop.

## Non-Clinical Staff Pack

Focus areas:

- Front desk privacy controls
- Service responsiveness and routing
- Data handling and verification
- Respectful communication at first contact

Starter scenarios:

1. Waiting Room Escalation
- Setup: Family frustration starts to spill into the lobby.
- Skill: Empathy plus a concrete next step.

2. Front Desk Privacy Check
- Setup: A caller requests protected details without clear authorization.
- Skill: Verify, redirect, and keep the exchange private.

3. Data Routing Error
- Setup: A message is sent to the wrong internal contact.
- Skill: Correct the route, notify the right owner, and document the fix.

## Leaders and Supervisors Pack

Focus areas:

- Speak-up culture and non-retaliation
- Fair conduct escalation
- Coaching in service failures
- Conflict and gift policy enforcement

Starter scenarios:

1. New Employee Observes Risk
- Setup: A new hire sees a policy violation and hesitates to report.
- Skill: Reinforce psychological safety and reporting pathways.

2. Silent Handling Request
- Setup: A manager asks to keep a reportable concern off the record.
- Skill: Redirect to formal escalation and protect the reporter.

3. Vendor Gift Boundary Check
- Setup: A gift arrives linked to a procurement relationship.
- Skill: Apply disclosure and conflict policy without delay.

## Monthly Refresher Format

- One role-specific scenario.
- One policy reminder.
- One manager follow-up question.
- One documented action for the next 30 days.

## Expansion Notes

- Add a fourth scenario when a department repeatedly misses the same behavior.
- Pair each scenario with the existing annual module it reinforces.
- Keep the wording short and action-oriented so the refresher works on mobile.
