# Scenario Bank (Starter Set)

## Service Excellence

1. Waiting Room Escalation
- Setup: Family upset by delays in updates.
- Skill: Empathy + specific next step.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "If we solve wait time with invisible cloaks, call IT immediately."

2. Handoff Breakdown
- Setup: Shift handoff missed key details.
- Skill: Closed-loop communication.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "Telepathy is still not in policy as a handoff method."

3. Service Recovery After Error
- Setup: Incorrect meal or non-critical request missed.
- Skill: Own, apologize, correct, follow-up.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "The phrase 'not my department' is not a recovery strategy."

4. Family Communication Tension
- Setup: Family wants updates every 10 minutes.
- Skill: Set expectations and communication rhythm.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "Hourly updates are good. Minute-by-minute live commentary is not required."

## Code of Conduct

5. Hallway Confidentiality
- Setup: Protected patient details discussed publicly.
- Skill: Immediate respectful intervention.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "If visitors can hear it, it belongs in a private space."

6. Gift and Vendor Influence
- Setup: Expensive gift linked to procurement outcomes.
- Skill: Disclosure and policy-based response.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "If it comes with a bow and a conflict, it is not a free lunch."

7. Documentation Integrity
- Setup: Team pressure to chart incomplete action.
- Skill: Accurate records and escalation.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "Creative writing belongs in novels, not charts."

8. Respectful Workplace
- Setup: Repeated inappropriate jokes.
- Skill: Address behavior and report patterns.
- Wrong-turn humor cue: "'Just kidding' is not a compliance exemption."

## Mixed Advanced Scenarios

9. Social Media Boundary Check
- Setup: Staff selfie reveals patient board in background.
- Skill: Privacy awareness and immediate correction.

10. Fast-Paced Weekend Shift
- Setup: Short staffing plus elevated patient frustration.
- Skill: Prioritization and transparent communication.

11. New Employee Observes Risk
- Setup: New hire sees policy violation and hesitates to report.
- Skill: Psychological safety + reporting channels.

12. Supervisor Conflict Moment
- Setup: Manager requests silent handling of a reportable concern.
- Skill: Non-retaliation protections and formal escalation.

## Assessment Mapping
- Each scenario maps to one policy objective.
- Minimum passing requirement: 80 percent on scenario accuracy.
- Any critical failure scenario triggers required remediation content.
