# Annual Training Blueprint

## Program Goal
Deliver a high-engagement annual training for all Destint Springs Healthcare staff that strengthens Service Excellence and Code of Conduct behaviors using interactive, scenario-driven learning.

## Audience Segments
- Clinical staff
- Non-clinical support staff
- Leaders and supervisors
- New hires using annual module as onboarding refresher

## Duration
- Total run time: 45 to 60 minutes
- Core module: 35 to 45 minutes
- Optional role-specific expansion: 10 to 15 minutes

## Learning Objectives
- Demonstrate patient-centered communication in difficult service moments.
- Apply confidentiality and conduct standards in daily operations.
- Identify and escalate ethics, safety, and policy concerns correctly.
- Make compliant decisions under time pressure.

## Experience Design
- Luxury software visual language with glassmorphism, layered gradients, and texture.
- Narrative framing: "The Excellence Vault" annual mission.
- Humor tone: professional, light, and respectful.
- Interaction cadence: every 30 to 60 seconds a click, choice, or challenge.

## Module Architecture
1. Welcome Cinematic (2 to 3 min)
- Why this annual training matters now.
- How points, badges, and mission progress work.

2. Service Excellence Studio (10 to 12 min)
- Empathy, ownership, communication loops, and service recovery.
- Branching scenarios with instant coaching feedback.

3. Code of Conduct Compass (12 to 15 min)
- Respectful workplace behavior.
- Privacy and confidentiality.
- Conflicts of interest.
- Integrity in documentation and reporting pathways.

4. Scenario Arena (10 to 15 min)
- Mixed real-world events by role.
- Choose, review impact, replay alternate choices.

5. Lightning Compliance Round (5 to 7 min)
- Timed question series reinforcing essentials.

6. Final Certification Checkpoint (4 to 6 min)
- Comprehensive scored assessment.
- Acknowledgment statement and completion status.

## Scoring Model
- Scenario quality points: 0 to 20 each.
- Streak bonus for consecutive best-practice actions.
- Badge unlocks for high-quality performance in trust, safety, and compliance.
- Certification tiers:
  - Gold: 85+
  - Silver: 65 to 84
  - Bronze: below 65 with required remediation steps

## Compliance Controls
- Policy citation attached to each scenario.
- Version stamp on all policy-derived content.
- Mandatory annual acknowledgment capture.
- Retake logic for failing score thresholds.

## Accessibility and Inclusion
- Keyboard navigation and visible focus states.
- Captioned media and transcript support.
- Color contrast and plain-language summaries.
- Mobile and desktop responsive layouts.

## LMS and Analytics
- Publish as SCORM 2004 or xAPI package.
- Capture completion, score, attempts, module time, and weak-topic trends.
- Dashboard views by role, department, and manager.

## Annual Update Workflow
- Month 1: gather policy updates and incidents data.
- Month 2: refresh scenarios and approve with HR/compliance.
- Month 3: pilot with cross-functional sample and launch.
