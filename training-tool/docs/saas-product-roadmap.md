# NyxCollective SaaS Product Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Current Foundation
- Premium interactive training frontend
- Backend for attempts, events, completion, analytics
- Multi-tenant data model
- SCORM-compatible client runtime

## Phase 2 - Commercial Readiness
- Tenant branding system (logos, colors, typography themes)
- Admin portal for uploading custom decks and policies
- LMS connector templates (Docebo, Cornerstone, Workday)
- Certificate templates and PDF generation

## Phase 3 - Enterprise Features
- SSO (SAML/OIDC)
- Department and manager hierarchy sync
- Role-based assignment rules
- AI-assisted scenario authoring and policy rewriting assistant

## Phase 4 - Monetization
- Subscription plans by learner volume
- Annual contract billing with seat true-up
- White-label add-on pricing
- Premium analytics add-on

## Phase 5 - Defensibility
- Benchmarking analytics across anonymous cohorts
- Skills heatmaps and targeted microlearning recommendations
- Continuous compliance updates and regulation packs
