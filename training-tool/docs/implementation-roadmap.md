# Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 1: Content Consolidation (Week 1)
- Extract and tag slides from both current decks by topic.
- Remove duplicates and outdated policy references.
- Convert long policy text into short action-oriented cards.

## Phase 2: Experience Build (Week 2 to 3)
- Build premium UI and interaction framework.
- Add scenario engine, scoring, and badge logic.
- Add timed challenge, checkpoint feedback, and summary view.

## Phase 3: Compliance and Leadership Review (Week 4)
- HR and compliance validate policy accuracy.
- Leadership validates service behavior standards.
- Finalize acknowledgment language and escalation references.

## Phase 4: LMS Integration (Week 5)
- Package for SCORM/xAPI.
- Configure completion/pass/fail rules.
- Validate tracking fields in LMS sandbox.

## Phase 5: Pilot and Launch (Week 6)
- Pilot with 15 to 25 users from multiple departments.
- Measure completion, confusion points, and engagement.
- Apply final fixes and publish organization-wide.

## Production Enhancements After MVP
- Voiceover narration by leadership and patient-experience champions.
- Role-based scenario packs by department.
- Monthly micro-refreshers to keep annual content fresh.
- Manager toolkit with coaching discussion guide.
