# No PHI / BAA Position Statement

This product is designed and positioned as a workforce training platform that does not require Protected Health Information (PHI) to deliver its core workflow.

## Intended Data Scope

The platform is intended to process training and operational metadata such as:

- learner name and work email
- role track and department context
- completion, score, attempt, and event analytics
- attestation status and training timestamps

The platform is not intended to store patient charts, diagnoses, medical record numbers, or treatment documentation.

## BAA Position

If a deployment does not create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI, a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is generally not required for this product workflow.

If a customer requests workflows that introduce PHI handling, legal and security review is required before deployment scope is approved.

## Customer and Operator Responsibilities

- configure implementation to avoid PHI entry in training workflows
- avoid entering PHI into free-text fields, support tickets, or exported notes
- restrict user-generated content to training and compliance context
- escalate any accidental PHI submission to the security contact process

## Accidental PHI Handling Procedure (Minimum)

1. Identify and isolate the affected records.
2. Notify designated security/legal contacts.
3. Remove or redact affected data where feasible.
4. Document incident timeline and corrective controls.

## Contracting and Procurement Note

This statement supports pre-procurement scoping discussions and is not legal advice.

Final BAA determination should be made by the customer's legal/compliance team based on the contracted deployment scope.
